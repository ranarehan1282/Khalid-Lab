Principal Investigator

RRK

Dr. Rana Rehan Khalid

Principal Investigator  ·  Assistant Professor

Dr. Rana Rehan Khalid is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Biosciences at Quaid-i-Azam University, Islamabad. He received his PhD in computational structural biology and completed postdoctoral training focused on protein dynamics and NMR-guided modelling.

His research group investigates the structural and dynamic basis of signalling protein function — with a particular emphasis on nitric oxide signalling (hsGC, HNOX domains), mutation effects in disease-linked proteins (PDE6, PKG-Iα), and the application of AI and bioinformatics to drug discovery and proteomics. He has published over 20 peer-reviewed articles (h-index 11, 416 citations).

Join Our Team

Khalid Lab welcomes motivated students and researchers at all career stages. We have a collaborative, supportive culture and offer training in modern computational structural biology tools and methods.

  • PhD positions: Open positions for HEC-funded PhD students (Pakistani nationals). Background in bioinformatics, biochemistry, molecular biology, or computational chemistry preferred.
  • Postdoctoral fellows: Candidates with a strong publication record in structural biology, MD simulations, or drug discovery are encouraged to apply with a CV and research statement.
  • MSc/MPhil rotations: QAU students interested in a research rotation should contact us directly.
  • Visiting researchers: International collaborators and visiting scientists are welcome for short stays.
