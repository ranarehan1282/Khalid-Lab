Welcome to Khalid Lab

Research in Bioinformatics, Structural Biology, and Protein Dynamics

We focus on understanding protein structures, mutations, and dynamics using computational and experimental methods. Our lab combines NMR, bioinformatics, and AI-based approaches to solve cutting-edge biological problems.

Research Areas

Protein Dynamics

Mutation Analysis

Predicting the effects of mutations on protein structure, stability, and function using computational tools and datasets.

Bioinformatics Tools

Developing software and pipelines for structural biology, chemical shift prediction, and NMR data analysis.

Working with experimental labs and research groups to integrate computational and experimental findings.
